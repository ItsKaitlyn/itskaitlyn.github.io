~ About ~

Random Time Attack 2 (RTA2 for short), is a mod all about speedrunning through very small rooms,
which are shuffled every time you play.
You'll always get the same rooms, just in a random order!

There are also ports of this mod to the Nintendo Wii, and the Nintendo Switch!
